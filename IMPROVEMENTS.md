# 🚀 Quick Start Guide - New Features

## What's New in Version 2.0?

This guide will help you get the most out of the latest enhancements to TryGC Hub Manager.

---

## 👤 1. Your Personal Profile

### Access Your Profile
1. Click the **user icon** in the top-right corner of the header
2. You'll see your personal performance dashboard

### What You'll Find
- **Performance Overview** - 4 key metrics at a glance
  - Tasks Completed (total and this month)
  - On-Time Delivery Rate (%)
  - Quality Score (0-100)
  - Average Task Duration (hours)

- **Performance Alerts** - See if you need to focus on something
  - Quality score too low?
  - Too many blocked tasks?
  - Handovers pending?

- **Detailed Metrics**
  - Task status breakdown (In Progress, Blocked)
  - Handover activity (acknowledgement rate)
  - Recent task history with statuses

## 🔐 2. Enhanced Security

### New Password Requirements
When signing up, your password must have:
- ✅ At least 8 characters
- ✅ At least 1 uppercase letter (A-Z)
- ✅ At least 1 lowercase letter (a-z)
- ✅ At least 1 number (0-9)

### Password Strength Indicator
During signup, you'll see a **password strength meter** showing:
- 🔴 **Weak** - Needs more complexity
- 🟡 **Medium** - Getting better
- 🟢 **Strong** - Excellent, ready to submit

### Real-Time Validation
Form fields now give you immediate feedback:
- ❌ Shows what's missing
- ✅ Confirms valid entries
- 💡 Provides helpful suggestions

## 📋 3. Better Task Management

### Improved Task Creation
When creating a task, you'll notice:

1. **Better Form Validation**
   - Real-time error messages
   - Field validation before submission
   - Helpful suggestions (e.g., "High priority but due date is 30 days away")

2. **Auto-Suggestions**
   - Estimated hours recommendations
   - Definition of Done (DOD) ideas
   - Similar task references

3. **Task Complexity Indicator**
   - Shows if task is Low/Medium/High complexity
   - Helps estimate completion time
   - Suggests breaking down complex tasks

### Task Quality Tips
- ✅ Use clear, descriptive titles (3+ characters)
- ✅ Add details to explain the full scope
- ✅ Define acceptance criteria (DOD items)
- ✅ Estimate the time required
- ✅ Set realistic due dates

## 🤝 4. Improved Handover Process

### Enhanced Handover Creation
New features help smooth handovers:

1. **Handover Readiness Score**
   - Shows you if the handover is ready (0-100%)
   - Missing info? You'll see what's needed

2. **Task Suggestions**
   - System suggests tasks that should be handed over
   - Shows task status and priority
   - Highlights any blocked or high-priority items

3. **Quality Checks**
   - Warns about tasks in other pending handovers
   - Detects duplicate handovers
   - Estimates handover completion time

4. **Better Watchouts**
   - Remind yourself to add important notes
   - These appear in the handover preview
   - Help incoming team members understand context

### Handover Best Practices
- ✅ Include all relevant tasks
- ✅ Add detailed watchouts/notes
- ✅ Mention any blocked items and why
- ✅ Highlight high-priority work
- ✅ Ensure readiness score > 70%

## ❓ FAQ

### Q: Why am I seeing validation errors?
**A:** The system now validates all inputs to prevent issues. Check the error message - it tells you exactly what's needed.

### Q: How is my quality score calculated?
**A:** It's based on your on-time delivery rate (70%) and task completion rate without blocking (30%).

### Q: Can I improve my performance metrics?
**A:** Yes! Complete tasks on time, avoid blocking, and communicate through handovers.

### Q: What does "handover readiness" mean?
**A:** It's a score (0-100%) showing if your handover has all necessary information. Higher is better.

### Q: Why are some tasks suggested for my handover?
**A:** The system suggests tasks you own that aren't completed yet. Review and add relevant ones.

### Q: How do I access my profile?
**A:** Click the **user icon** (👤) in the top-right corner of any page.

## 🆘 Troubleshooting

### Issue: Form won't submit
**Solution**: Check for red error messages. Fix each field and try again.

### Issue: Password too weak
**Solution**: Add a capital letter, lowercase letter, and number. At least 8 characters total.

### Issue: Can't find profile button
**Solution**: Look in the top-right header. The user icon should be next to the "New Entry" button.

### Issue: Performance metrics seem wrong
**Solution**: Metrics update when tasks are created/updated. Wait a moment and refresh.
